Our Story

David and Meredith began their journey together sophomore year of high school through band. Meredith, an accomplished mallet player, and David, a strapping young saxophone artist, enjoyed spending days with friends, playing games, scooping ice cream, and playing music. David and Meredith have spent many milestones together such as learning how to drive a car, graduating high school, renting our first apartments, graduating college, beginning our careers and most recently adopting Blue. We look forward to spending this day with those of you who have supported us along the way and will continue to support us as we grow together.
